Official UNO Rulebook

Rules Overview

Be the first player to discard all cards in your hand. Match cards by color, value, or action symbol corresponding to the active card on the discard pile.

Special Cards

  • Skip: Next player is skipped.
  • Reverse: Reverses play direction.
  • Draw Two (+2): Next player draws 2 cards and skips their turn.
  • Wild Card: Permits change of active color. Playable on any turn.
  • Wild Draw Four (+4): Player selects next active color; next opponent draws 4 cards and is skipped.

Calling "UNO!"

When down to 1 card in hand, click the **UNO!** button. If you omit this and an opponent catches you within 2 seconds using **CATCH!**, you are hit with a 2-card draw penalty!
